Champions League

All Titles

1967-1968: Manchester United won their first European Cup (now known as the UEFA Champions League) during the 1967-1968 season. They defeated Benfica in the final after extra time, with a score of 4-1.
1998-1999: Manchester United secured their second UEFA Champions League title during the 1998-1999 season. They famously won the final against Bayern Munich with two late goals, securing a 2-1 victory.
2007-2008: Manchester United won their third UEFA Champions League title during the 2007-2008 season. They defeated Chelsea in a dramatic penalty shootout after the match had ended 1-1 in regulation time.
